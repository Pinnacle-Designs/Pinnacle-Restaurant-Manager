02

The problem

Restaurants run on fragmented tools — and owners fly blind on profit

  • POS doesn't talk to inventory — recipe depletion and theoretical food cost stay manual
  • Menu lives in six places — POS, delivery apps, website, and tableside QR drift out of sync
  • Labor & payroll are spreadsheets — scheduling, punches, holiday rules, and compliance live in silos
  • Back office is reactive — invoices, POs, and vendor credits reconciled after the fact
  • Analytics require exports — owners can't ask "what's hurting profit this week?" in plain English
  • Multi-location groups lack one view — every store is a separate island of tools and reports
Independent restaurants lose 10–15+ hours/week reconciling numbers instead of running the floor — while margin leaks go unnoticed until month-end.
