Back of house

Kitchen that keeps up with the rush

KDS & routing

Kitchen display shows fired tickets by station. Orders move from pending → in prep → ready without paper chits.

Prep & production

Kitchen and BOH modules track prep lists, production batches, and back-of-house workflows alongside the menu.

  • Fire & recall — control ticket timing from the POS; kitchen sees updates instantly
  • Recipe-linked depletion — sold items drive theoretical inventory usage for food cost accuracy
  • Log book — shift notes and handoffs stay in the same system as orders and labor
